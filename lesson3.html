Gender-bending: nouns with dual gender

There are quite a few exceptions to the general rule on the endings for masculine and feminine nouns. We will examine some of those exceptions in this lesson.

Masculine and feminine nouns with varying meanings

There are some nouns in Spanish that have corresponding masculine and feminine variations. However, the meaning of both is completely different.

La Paz es la capital de Bolivia

La Paz is the capital city of Bolivia

El capital de la compañía

The company’s capital

Capital

Capital is a feminine noun when it refers to the capital city, but it is masculine when it refers to the money a company has.

We will spend a bit more time on these in a future lesson.

Names of animals

The gender of animals can sometimes be specified by changing the final -o for the males into an -a for the females.

Rango es el perro y Pin-Pin es la perra

Rango is the male dog and Pin-Pin is the female dog

La gata de Carla es marrón

Carla’s female cat is brown

However, some animals have completely different forms for the males and females.

La vaca es grande

The cow is big

El toro es negro

The bull is black

El caballo es rápido

The horse is fast

La yegua es lenta

The mare is slow

Don’t try to predict the gender of all types of nouns beforehand. If a noun does not belong to any of the above categories, you will need to memorize the gender as you learn that noun. Over time, it will become more natural and easier to determine the gender of a noun, but keep an eye out for the ones that are exceptions to the rules.

El intérprete de inglés es americano

The English interpreter is American

La intérprete de francés es francesa

The French interpreter is French

Esta vaca es alta

This cow is tall

Este bull es alto

This toro is tall

Un habitante del pueblo es hablador 

An inhabitant of the village is talkative

María es una habitante de este país 

María is an inhabitant of this country

Él es el testigo del crimen

He is the witness of the crime

La testigo es importante

The witness is important
